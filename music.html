HOW TO ADD YOUR MUSIC
1. Upload your song to Spotify, SoundCloud, or YouTube
2. Copy the embed link from the share menu
3. Open music.html and find the track you want to update
4. Replace the waveform div with your iframe embed
5. Save the file — your player will appear automatically
